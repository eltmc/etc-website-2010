Recommended

We asked the committee what books they liked and found useful. Some books are specifically for multiples and others are just good general parenting guides. There are also some of our favourite children's books. If you buy any of the books using the Amazon links, the club gets a small percentage.

Pregnancy - Both serious and fun reads to guide you through your pregnancy.

Baby - Parenting guides focused on the baby stage

Toddler - Parenting guides focused on the toddler stage

Child - Parenting guides to raising children

Feeding - Cookery books for everything from weaning to family meals

Books for children - Our favourite story books for babies, toddlers and children

General - General interest books involving multiples
